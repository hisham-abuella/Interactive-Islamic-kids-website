ğŸŒŸ Why is Surah Al-Fatiha Special?

Surah Al-Fatiha is the very first chapter of the Holy Quran. The meaning of "Fatiha" is "The Opening" - so this is the opening of the Quran!

Muslims recite this beautiful surah in every single rakah (unit) of prayer - that means we say it at least 17 times every day in our five daily prayers!

This surah also summarizes the whole Quran, so it's also called "Umm Al-Quran" (The Mother of the Quran)!
